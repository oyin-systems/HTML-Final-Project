About Me!

Welcome to my world! I'm a Software Developer with a passion for Frontend Development. By day, you'll find me coding, and did you know that I once hated coding? By night, I transform into a Fashion Enthusiast.

I strongly believe in the power of love and optimism. When I'm not working, you can catch me listening to music, or watching movies or playing board games, which include Scrabble and Chess. I love good food, and here's a fun fact-I enjoy trying out new things in the kitchen.

My motto is "Be the Energy You Want to Attract", and I'm always up for hangouts and movie dates. Let's connect and share our stories!
